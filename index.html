Boarding Policy

Ensuring a safe and healthy stay for all our guests.

1. HEALTH & SAFETY

  • Cats must be healthy and free from contagious diseases.
  • Vaccinations should be up to date.
  • Ongoing medical conditions must be informed in advance.
  • We may refuse boarding for cats showing parasites or aggressive behavior.

2. PET PARENT VERIFICATION

  • Valid current address proof of the pet parent is mandatory.
  • Required for security and emergency contact.

3. BOARDING ENVIRONMENT

  • Clean, calm, indoor, cat-only environment.
  • Regular feeding, litter cleaning, and hygiene maintenance.

4. SUPERVISION & CARE

  • Multiple daily visits for feeding, cleaning, and health checks.
  • Continuous monitoring and scheduled checks are followed.
