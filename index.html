Boarding Policy

Please read our policy carefully to ensure a calm and hygienic experience for every guest.

1. HEALTH & SAFETY

  • Cats must be healthy and free from contagious diseases.
  • Vaccinations should be up to date.
  • Ongoing medical conditions or allergies must be informed in advance.
  • We may refuse boarding for cats showing parasites or aggressive behavior.

2. PET PARENT VERIFICATION

  • Valid current address proof of the pet parent is mandatory.
  • Required for security, emergency contact, and record-keeping.

3. BOARDING ENVIRONMENT

  • Clean, calm, indoor, cat-only environment.
  • Regular feeding, litter cleaning, and hygiene maintenance.

4. SUPERVISION & CARE

  • Multiple daily visits for feeding, cleaning, and health checks.
  • Continuous monitoring and scheduled checks are followed.

5. FREE ROAMING POLICY

  • Friendly cats may get free access to the home after 24–48 hours of observation.
  • Decision is taken solely by us based on adaptability and safety.

8. PICKUP & DROP FACILITY

  • Free up to 10 km for: Single cat (Min 5 days) / Multiple cats (Min 3 days).
  • Beyond 10 km or shorter stays may be chargeable.

10. EMERGENCY CARE

  • Parents informed immediately in case of medical emergency.
  • Medical expenses incurred will be borne by the pet parent.

11. DISCLAIMER

While all care is taken, Purring Cat Boarding is not responsible for stress-related issues, pre-existing conditions, or unforeseen health events.
